    • Hi there! Thanks for stopping by and checking out my work. I've been playing around with photography for about 2 years now and it has truly turned into a passion. I experienced my first taste of a digital camera when I attended First Institute for videography and editing in 2023. I started experimenting with photography and haven't stopped. I started off doing a lot of landscapes, then as I got brave I started doing anything and everything! I especially love experimenting with portraits and bringing out the best in people. I also love taking pictures of food and pets. My goal is to continue to learn and grow. I hope you enjoy my work as much as I enjoy creating it. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to book a session!
